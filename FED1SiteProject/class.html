Class Subjects

This intro breaks down the basics of the forex market. What is Forex? Who trades Forex and why you should trade.

Now that you know the basics we will go over fundeamental and technical analysis to better understand how the market moves.
How to identify support and resistance levels, popular indicators, moving averages and intro to Japanese CandleSticks
The ability to identify high probablity trade setups are vital in the markets. Knowing when to get in the market and when to get out can be a defining wheather youre a profitable trader or a gambler.
This is for the seasoned trader. We will take a deeper dive into multiple time frame analysis, Harmonic Patterns, trading divergences binary options and much more.
